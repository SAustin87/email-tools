How-to guide

Adding modules

When hovering over one of the modules to the right, click the + icon to add to the your email build.

Copying modules

To copy the code for individual modules, click the copy icon when hovering over the module of your choice.

Editing modules

All elements within modules are editable. Clicking on text, images or buttons will open an editing suite, giving you options to change text, style, image links, hyperlink urls and more. You can also remove this element entirely.

Deleting modules

To delete modules, you must click the “remove modules” button. This opens a menu, listing current modules. Double clicking on those modules you wish to remove will delete them.

Share Link

The share link button copies a shareable link to your clipboard.

Copy code

This copies your full email build to your clipboard, including head tags, styles, and all body content.

Download file

Downloads a named HTML file of the full email.
